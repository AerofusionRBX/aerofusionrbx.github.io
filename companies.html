Fusion Group Companies

  • Aerofusion
  • FranceConnect
  • Fusion Handling
  • FusionBus

Aerofusion is the largest Fusion Group company. Aerofusion is an airline based in Spain and England with headquarters in both. Aerofusion was founded in July 2020, they offer a premium service called Speedy Boarding, with Speedy Boarding you get to board the plane before Economy passengers and also you get the first pick of the seats.

FranceConnect is another airline of the Fusion Group that was founded in August 2020, they are based in Paris, France, they operate flights on behalf of Aerofusion sometimes. They also carry over the same speedy boarding scheme from Aerofusion.

Fusion Handling is a airport ground handling service, they serve as the ground crew service for all the Fusion Group airlines, they are an extension of the Orange Alliance ground team, meaning all staff are not directly employed by us but by the Orange Alliance which we are a member of.

FusionBus is a bus company operating across Europe, mostly at holiday destinations for shuttling passengers to and from their hotels when they are on holiday. All passengers on a Fusion Group package holiday may find their self onboard one of our luxury FusionBus coaches.
